Laser Tag

Unleash your adventurous spirit in Epic Entertainment's premier two-story Laser Tag arena! This state-of-the-art facility takes family fun to new heights, presenting an electrifying challenge that both kids and adults will love.

In our dynamic, multi-level playground, you'll dive into a world where futuristic fun meets thrilling strategy. Neon lights illuminate the battlefield as you navigate around the obstacles, ramp up the scores, and claim victory. Whether you're weaving through the upper deck or sneaking in the shadows below, every corner offers a new twist in this epic game of light.

Packed with advanced laser tag technology, our equipment provides an ultra-responsive and immersive experience. With comfortable, lightweight vests and high-precision phasers, you'll feel the rush of every tag, every dodge, every win. And with a wide range of game modes to choose from, there's a new challenge waiting each time you step into the arena.

Perfect for family outings, birthday parties, or team-building events, Epic Entertainment's two-story Laser Tag is more than just a game—it's an unforgettable journey. Prepare for an adrenaline-fueled experience that'll leave you craving for another round. Aim high, think fast, and let the laser tag adventure begin at Epic Entertainment!

Must be 4 years old

Hologate Virtual Reality

What is Hologate?
Hologate is an award winning 4 person interactive virtual reality attraction that takes gaming to a new dimension. When you put on the VR headset, you are immediately transported to a new world. In this world, you could be battling robots and dragons, having a snowball fight with your friends, or fighting off a hoard of oncoming zombies.
Hologate is a physically engaging, immersive experience that’s suitable for players of any age. The advanced graphic technology creates a realistic environment with zero motion sickness. This VR system boasts multiple games, all of which are exclusive to the Hologate platform. These exciting games are designed for gamers of all skills levels; they are easy to learn and difficult to master. Keep playing to discover new worlds, obtain the high score, and earn bragging rights among your friends.

Must be 4 years old
